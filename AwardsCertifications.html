ISO 27001:2022 Certification
Information security, cybersecurity and privacy protection ISO/IEC 27001:2022 is an international standard that provides a systematic framework for managing information security within an organization. Grand Ortus implemented all necessary steps to comply with this standard. Accredited and authorised auditors performed assessments of the organisation’s ISMS to confirm its complete compliance with the ISO 27001:2022 requirements. The ISO 27001:2022 award certifies that Grand Ortus can thoroughly evaluate or identify risks and that appropriate measures are in place to handle or minimise them. The breadth of Grand Ortus certification ensures continuity of data stewardship through the entire service delivery process. With this certification, Grand Ortus clients can rest assured that they are working with a reliable Information Technology Development & service provider dedicated to protecting the integrity of their sensitive data and operating according to internationally acknowledged guidelines.
ISO 9001:2015 Certification
Certification for Quality Management System The ISO 9001 family applies to organisations wanting to improve the quality of their service to consistently meet their customer’s needs and expectations, addressing various aspects of quality management. Quality is critical to everything we do. Our ISO 9001:2015 certificate is one way to show our commitment to delivering the highest level of service. Obtaining an ISO 9001 certification means that we have properly demonstrated the ability to meet regulatory and industry requirements and have implemented a protocol that meets quality management system standards. It provides a complete management process that allows us to make constant improvements within our system. Our ISO certification allows us to deliver outstanding services to our customers, helping us achieve a much higher level of customer satisfaction, as the ISO quality management standards are present in each step of our value chain.
